Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common things we get asked.

Is Well Care Spendables a bank?

No, we are not a bank. We are a financial technology company that provides tools to help you analyze your spending and manage your financial wellness. We securely connect to your existing bank accounts to provide insights but do not hold your money directly.

How do you make money?

Our business model is simple: we offer a free tier with core features and a premium subscription for advanced analytics, personalized insights, and priority support. We do not sell your personal financial data to third parties.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. Security is our top priority. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) for your data at rest and in transit. We also require multi-factor authentication and adhere to strict data privacy policies to ensure your information is always protected.

Can I cancel my account at any time?

Yes, you are in complete control. You can close your account and delete your data at any time directly from your account settings. When you delete your account, we permanently remove your personal information from our systems.
